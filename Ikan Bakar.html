IKAN BAKAR PARAMESWARA

When you arrive in Malacca, don't forget to stop by to eat at the Ikan Bakar Parameswara which is quite special with their seafood-based cuisine. You can choose fish or any other seafood you want and weigh it, after considering choosing the type of cuisine that will be cooked according to the cuisine you like.
