ROTI CANAI KAYU ARANG

Don't know what to eat for breakfast in Malacca? Let's start our day with Roti Canai Kayu Arang. The location is Kampung Bakar Batu, Bachang. The stall serves a variety of traditional breakfast such as Roti Canai, Lontong, Nasi Lemak, and many more. The main course here is their Roti Canai Kayu Arang. The name comes from the fact that all roti canai are cooked with charcoal wood.
