STORYLINE

Jake, a student, is alone within an abandoned school. Jake was in fear. He is trying to escape. There is a method for him to leave the school, though. One of them is to finish the missions in each room related to the Korean language. Jake has absolutely no language skills in Korean. Jake will practise his Hangul vocabulary in this game and answer questions regarding to it. Jake needs your assistance to escape the school. We need to help jake.
